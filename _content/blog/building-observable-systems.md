Observability is essential for running production microservices. Here's how to build comprehensive monitoring infrastructure that provides visibility into every layer of your stack.

## The Three Pillars

Complete observability requires three complementary approaches: metrics with Prometheus, logs with structured logging, and traces with OpenTelemetry. Each provides different insights, and together they give you complete visibility into system behavior.

## Why It Matters

You can't fix what you can't see. Instrument from day one, not after the first incident. The investment in observability pays for itself the first time you debug a production issue in minutes instead of hours.
